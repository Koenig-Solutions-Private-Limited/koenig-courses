## What You Will Learn
- Tailor PRINCE2 principles to agile environments using the five PRINCE2 Agile targets: protection, delivery, collaboration, transparency, and adaptability
- Assess project suitability for agility with the Agilometer tool and decide on fixing/flexing PRINCE2 elements
- Define and manage requirements through agile practices like MoSCoW prioritization and user stories within PRINCE2's product descriptions
- Apply PRINCE2 themes—such as business case, quality, and risk—in agile contexts, including lean startup and frequent releases
- Implement rich communication, self-organization, and agile behaviors to drive effective team performance and stakeholder engagement

## Who Should Attend
Project managers, programme managers, agile coaches, and team leads with PRINCE2 Foundation certification or equivalent experience, plus familiarity with agile methods like Scrum or Kanban. Ideal for those handling complex projects needing structured oversight.
