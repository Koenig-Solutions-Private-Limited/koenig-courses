## What You Will Learn
- Analyze system logs, including LTM, ASM, and AVR, to pinpoint root causes of failures
- Capture and interpret network traffic using tcpdump, ssldump, and Wireshark for protocol-level diagnostics
- Validate and troubleshoot virtual server, pool, and iRule configurations for optimal traffic management
- Diagnose high availability issues in device clusters, failover events, and sync problems
- Employ BIG-IQ and performance tools to monitor, alert, and tune BIG-IP deployments

## Who Should Attend
Network engineers, systems administrators, and F5 specialists with intermediate BIG-IP experience, such as LTM or ASM configuration knowledge. Suited for those transitioning to advanced support roles or preparing for production troubleshooting in enterprise settings.
