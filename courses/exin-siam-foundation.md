Demand for EXIN SIAM™ Foundation certified professionals surges as enterprises adopt multi-supplier models to optimize IT operations. ## What You Will Learn
- Grasp the SIAM ecosystem, including customer organizations, service integrators, and service providers
- Master SIAM principles such as collaboration, transparency, and continual improvement
- Examine SIAM processes across strategy, plan, design, transition, and operation phases
- Define key SIAM roles like process owner, service owner, and service integrator
- Understand SIAM governance frameworks and practical implementation roadmaps

## Who Should Attend
