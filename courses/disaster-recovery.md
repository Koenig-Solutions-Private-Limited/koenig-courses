## Why This Certification Matters
Demand for Disaster Recovery expertise surges as enterprises prioritize business continuity amid rising cyber risks and hybrid cloud complexities. Professionals certified in this area secure roles like DR specialists, infrastructure architects, and resilience engineers, often commanding higher salaries and leadership opportunities. It directly boosts career trajectories by demonstrating proven ability to mitigate outages, ensuring compliance and rapid recovery in high-stakes environments.

## What You Will Learn
- Formulating comprehensive disaster recovery plans aligned with organizational risk assessments and regulatory standards
- Configuring backup solutions using tools like Veeam or Azure Site Recovery for efficient data replication
- Calculating and optimizing Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO) for minimal impact
- Implementing high-availability clustering and automated failover across on-premises and cloud environments
- Conducting tabletop exercises, simulations, and post-recovery audits to validate and refine DR effectiveness

## Who Should Attend
System administrators, IT operations managers, cloud engineers, and cybersecurity analysts with 2–5 years of infrastructure experience will benefit most. It's suited for those transitioning into resilience roles or enhancing existing skills in hybrid/multi-cloud setups, regardless of vendor-specific backgrounds.
