## What You Will Learn
- Apply ISO 13053 guidelines to assess Six Sigma project structures, including Define and Measure phases for baseline performance.
- Conduct on-site audits of Analyze and Improve stages, using statistical tools to verify root cause analysis and solution efficacy.
- Evaluate Control phase implementations, focusing on sustainment metrics like process capability indices (Cpk) and control charts.
- Identify audit nonconformities in DMAIC cycles and recommend corrective actions based on ISO 13053 quantitative criteria.
- Develop audit reports with evidence-based findings, risk assessments, and follow-up verification protocols.
