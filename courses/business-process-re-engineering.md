## What You Will Learn
- Mapping as-is processes using BPMN notation and flowcharting tools for comprehensive visualization.
- Conducting root cause analysis with techniques like Pareto charts and fishbone diagrams to pinpoint inefficiencies.
- Designing to-be processes incorporating lean principles and automation integration.
- Developing change management plans, including stakeholder engagement and risk mitigation strategies.
- Measuring post-re-engineering performance via KPIs such as cycle time reduction and cost savings.
