## What You Will Learn
- Interpret GDPR's core principles, including lawfulness, fairness, and transparency in data processing.
- Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs) to identify and mitigate high-risk processing activities.
- Define the DPO's independence, advisory duties, and monitoring obligations under Article 39.
- Implement data subject rights, such as access, rectification, erasure, and portability requests.
- Develop incident response plans for breaches, including 72-hour notification timelines to supervisory authorities.
