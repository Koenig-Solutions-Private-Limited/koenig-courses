## What You Will Learn
- Importing CAD and BIM models from Revit, Rhino, or SketchUp into Unity for seamless scene setup
- Applying PBR materials, HDR lighting, and post-processing effects to achieve photorealistic architectural renders
- Building interactive walkthroughs with Unity's NavMesh and camera controls for client navigation
- Integrating Oculus or HTC Vive for VR-based architectural tours and fly-throughs
- Optimizing high-poly scenes using LOD systems, occlusion culling, and baking for performant real-time playback
