Ideal for operational technology (OT) professionals, it equips participants to secure critical infrastructure against evolving threats. In today's IT landscape, ICS-Scada expertise is vital as cyber incidents targeting energy, manufacturing, and utilities sectors rise, demanding skilled defenders to protect interconnected industrial environments.

## Why This Certification Matters
Demand for ICS-Scada specialists surges amid rising OT cyberattacks, with roles like SCADA engineers and ICS security analysts commanding premium salaries. This certification validates skills in safeguarding industrial networks, boosting employability in sectors like oil & gas, water treatment, and power generation, while positioning professionals for leadership in resilient infrastructure management.

## What You Will Learn
- Architect and deploy SCADA systems using protocols like Modbus, DNP3, and OPC for real-time industrial monitoring
- Identify ICS vulnerabilities through threat modeling and penetration testing tailored to PLCs and RTUs
- Implement segmentation and access controls to isolate OT networks from IT environments
- Develop incident response strategies for SCADA disruptions, including forensic analysis
- Conduct risk assessments and compliance audits aligned with standards like IEC 62443 and NIST SP 800-82

## Who Should Attend
Control systems engineers, OT cybersecurity analysts, industrial network administrators, and SCADA operators with 2+ years in automation or manufacturing IT. Suited for those transitioning from IT to OT security roles.
