# Lead SCADA Security Manager — Training & Certification Guide

The [Lead SCADA Security Manager](https://www.koenig-solutions.com/plc-and-scada-course-training?utm_source=github&utm_medium=backlink&utm_campaign=github-publisher) course equips professionals with advanced skills to protect industrial control systems (ICS) from cyber threats. It covers SCADA architecture, threat modeling, secure configurations for PLCs, and regulatory compliance. Ideal for cybersecurity experts in critical infrastructure sectors like energy, water, and manufacturing, this training addresses the rising attacks on operational technology (OT), where a Lead SCADA Security Manager role is essential for safeguarding automated processes in an increasingly connected IT/OT landscape.

## Why This Certification Matters
Demand for Lead SCADA Security Manager expertise surges amid escalating ICS cyberattacks, with roles like OT security leads, ICS engineers, and compliance officers commanding premium salaries. This certification validates your ability to mitigate risks in vital sectors, boosting career progression to senior positions and enhancing organizational resilience against disruptions.

## What You Will Learn
- Assess vulnerabilities in SCADA networks using tools like Nessus and ICS-specific scanners for protocol analysis
- Design and deploy segmentation strategies between IT and OT environments with firewalls and DMZs
- Secure PLC ladder logic programming against unauthorized modifications and injection attacks
- Develop and test incident response playbooks tailored to SCADA disruptions, including forensic techniques
- Ensure compliance with IEC 62443 standards through risk assessments and security lifecycle management

## Who Should Attend
Experienced security engineers, ICS analysts, OT managers, and control system engineers with 3+ years in industrial automation or cybersecurity. Suited for those transitioning to leadership in SCADA protection.
