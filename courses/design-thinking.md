## What You Will Learn
- Conducting empathy interviews and creating user personas to deeply understand stakeholder needs
- Crafting precise problem statements using "How Might We" frameworks for IT challenges
- Applying ideation techniques like brainstorming and SCAMPER to generate diverse solutions
- Designing low-fidelity prototypes with tools such as Figma or paper sketching
- Implementing iterative testing cycles with usability metrics and feedback loops

## Who Should Attend
