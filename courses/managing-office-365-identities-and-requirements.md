## What You Will Learn
- Plan and deploy Azure AD Connect for hybrid identity synchronization, including pass-through authentication and federation options
- Configure role-based access control (RBAC) and administrative units to delegate permissions effectively in Office 365 tenants
- Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA), conditional access policies, and identity protection features against common threats
- Manage user lifecycle processes, including provisioning, licensing, and self-service password reset (SSPR)
- Assess and fulfill Office 365 licensing, subscription, and domain requirements for optimal tenant deployment
