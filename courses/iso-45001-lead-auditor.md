Demand for ISO 45001 Lead Auditor certified professionals surges as industries prioritize worker safety amid regulatory pressures and incident reductions. ## What You Will Learn
- Interpret ISO 45001:2018 clauses, including context, leadership, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, and improvement.
- Apply ISO 19011 audit principles to develop comprehensive audit programs and checklists for OH&S systems.
- Conduct on-site audits using interviewing techniques, evidence collection, and risk-based sampling methods.
- Identify nonconformities, root causes, and opportunities for corrective actions during audit execution.
- Compile audit reports, present findings to top management, and lead follow-up verification activities.

## Who Should Attend
Safety managers, quality assurance professionals, internal auditors, and consultants with 2–5 years in occupational health or compliance. Suited for those seeking to transition into lead auditing roles or enhance existing audit capabilities in manufacturing, IT operations, or service sectors.
