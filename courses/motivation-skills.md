## What You Will Learn
- Apply Maslow's hierarchy and Herzberg's two-factor theory to diagnose motivation gaps in IT development teams
- Design personalized incentive programs using OKRs to align individual goals with sprint deliverables
- Implement active listening and recognition rituals to elevate morale during high-pressure release cycles
- Facilitate resilience workshops addressing imposter syndrome common among software engineers
- Measure motivation impact via surveys and KPIs, adjusting strategies for remote and hybrid IT setups
