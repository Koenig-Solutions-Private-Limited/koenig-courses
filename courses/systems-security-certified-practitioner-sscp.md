## What You Will Learn
- Implement and administer access controls using authentication, authorization, and accounting (AAA) mechanisms.
- Perform risk identification, monitoring, and analysis to prioritize threats across IT environments.
- Develop incident response and recovery plans, including forensic techniques and business continuity strategies.
- Apply cryptographic principles for data protection, including key management and public key infrastructure (PKI).
- Secure network communications and systems against vulnerabilities like malware and application exploits.
