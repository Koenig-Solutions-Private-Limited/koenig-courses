## What You Will Learn
- Apply South African skills development legislation, including NQF levels and SAQA requirements, to design compliant programs.
- Conduct learner and workplace skills assessments using validated tools and criteria.

- Develop and implement workplace skills plans and annual training reports for SETA submissions.
- Facilitate interactive learning sessions tailored to diverse adult learners in professional settings.
