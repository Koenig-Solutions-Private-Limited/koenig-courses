# ISO-22317 Foundation — Training & Certification Guide

The [ISO-22317 Foundation](https://www.koenig-solutions.com/iso-22317-foundation-training-certification-course?utm_source=github&utm_medium=backlink&utm_campaign=github-publisher) course provides foundational knowledge on ISO 22317, the international standard for business impact analysis (BIA) in business continuity management. It equips participants with skills to identify critical business functions, assess impacts of disruptions, and prioritize recovery strategies. Ideal for IT professionals, risk managers, and continuity planners, this certification is essential in today's IT landscape, where cyber threats, supply chain issues, and natural disasters demand robust resilience planning to minimize downtime and financial losses.

## Why This Certification Matters
Demand for ISO-22317 Foundation certified experts surges in industries facing frequent disruptions, such as finance, healthcare, and manufacturing. Relevant roles include business continuity coordinators and risk analysts, where this credential validates expertise in BIA fundamentals. It boosts career prospects by demonstrating commitment to ISO standards, opening doors to senior resilience positions and compliance-driven organizations.

## What You Will Learn
- Understand core principles of business impact analysis as defined in ISO 22317.
- Identify and categorize critical business activities and supporting resources.
- Perform qualitative and quantitative impact assessments for disruptions.
- Determine recovery time objectives (RTOs) and recovery point objectives (RPOs).
- Apply BIA outputs to develop prioritized continuity strategies.

## Who Should Attend
This course suits business continuity planners, risk assessment specialists, IT disaster recovery coordinators, and compliance officers with little to no prior BIA experience. It's also valuable for auditors and managers entering resilience fields.
