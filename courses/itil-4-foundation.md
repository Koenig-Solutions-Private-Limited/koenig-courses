# ITIL® 4 Foundation — Training & Certification Guide

The [ITIL® 4 Foundation](https://www.koenig-solutions.com/itil-foundation-certification-training-course?utm_source=github&utm_medium=backlink&utm_campaign=github-publisher) course delivers essential knowledge on modern IT service management, including the Service Value System, four dimensions of service management, guiding principles, and core practices. Designed for IT practitioners entering ITSM, it bridges traditional processes with agile and DevOps realities. In today's fast-evolving IT landscape, ITIL® 4 Foundation empowers professionals to create value-focused services, optimize workflows, and support digital transformation initiatives effectively.

## Why This Certification Matters
Demand for ITIL® 4 Foundation holders surges in service-oriented IT roles, such as ITSM coordinators and service delivery specialists. It validates skills in value co-creation, boosting employability amid cloud migrations and hybrid operations. Certified pros often secure promotions, higher salaries, and pathways to advanced ITIL specializations.

## What You Will Learn
- Grasp the ITIL service value system, including its holistic components for end-to-end value delivery
- Examine the four dimensions of service management: organizations, information/technology, partners/suppliers, and value streams
- Apply the seven ITIL guiding principles, like focus on value and think holistically, to real-world decisions
- Map service value chain activities, from engage to plan/improve, and their interconnections
- Recognize the purpose, key terms, and components of 15 ITIL practices across general, service, and technical categories

## Who Should Attend
IT service desk analysts, support technicians, junior IT managers, business analysts, and process coordinators with minimal ITSM experience. Ideal for newcomers aiming to formalize service management basics.
