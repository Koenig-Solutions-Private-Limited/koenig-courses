## What You Will Learn
- Formulate schedule strategies that align with project goals, stakeholder needs, and organizational constraints
- Build and baseline detailed schedules using critical path method (CPM), PERT, and resource leveling techniques

- Execute schedule closeout processes, including audits, lessons learned, and final reporting
- Integrate professional responsibility principles, ethics, and compliance in all scheduling activities
