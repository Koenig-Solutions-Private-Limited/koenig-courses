## What You Will Learn
- Configure vManage, vSmart, and vBond controllers for initial SD-WAN fabric onboarding and zero-touch provisioning.
- Design and apply centralized data, control, and OMP policies to enforce segmentation and QoS across VPNs.

- Troubleshoot control plane issues with OMP debugging, BFD sessions, and vEdge/cEdge validation logs.
