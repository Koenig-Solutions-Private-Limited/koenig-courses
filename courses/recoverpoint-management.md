## What You Will Learn
- Deploy and initialize RecoverPoint clusters, including splitter integration with storage arrays like VMAX and XtremIO
- Configure replication sets, enabling CDP, CRR, and CLR modes with custom SLAs and RPO targets
- Manage journal volumes and image access for point-in-time recovery during testing or failover
- Execute Metro DR and fan-out replication scenarios using Unisphere for RecoverPoint
- Troubleshoot performance issues via system logs, alerts, and hardware diagnostics
