# JBPM — Training & Certification Guide

The [JBPM](https://www.koenig-solutions.com/jbpm-training-online?utm_source=github&utm_medium=backlink&utm_campaign=github-publisher) course equips participants with hands-on skills in jBPM, the open-source business process management suite built on Java. It delves into process modeling, execution engines, and integration with Drools rules engine, ideal for developers and analysts automating complex workflows. In today's IT landscape, JBPM stands out for enabling scalable BPM solutions in enterprises undergoing digital transformation, reducing manual processes and enhancing operational efficiency.

## Why This Certification Matters
JBPM expertise drives demand in workflow automation roles like BPM developers and process engineers, where organizations seek to optimize business operations. Holding JBPM skills boosts career progression, opening doors to senior positions in fintech, healthcare, and manufacturing, with professionals often commanding higher salaries due to proven abilities in process orchestration.

## What You Will Learn
- Model and simulate business processes using BPMN 2.0 standards within the jBPM designer tool
- Configure and deploy the jBPM runtime engine for process execution and persistence
- Implement human task services and custom work item handlers for user interactions
- Integrate jBPM with Drools for decision automation and complex event processing
- Expose and manage process APIs via REST endpoints and Kie Server

## Who Should Attend
Java developers, business process analysts, and solution architects with intermediate programming experience. Suited for those transitioning to BPM roles or enhancing enterprise workflow skills, particularly in teams handling automation projects.
