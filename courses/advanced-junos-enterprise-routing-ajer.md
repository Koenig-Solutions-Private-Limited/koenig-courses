## What You Will Learn
- Configure and optimize OSPF and IS-IS for multi-area enterprise deployments, including demand circuits and overload protection
- Implement BGP route reflection, confederations, and advanced policy controls for scalable peering
- Design Class-of-Service (CoS) hierarchies with classifiers, queues, and schedulers for QoS assurance
- Deploy IP multicast routing using PIM sparse and dense modes, with Anycast RP for efficiency
- Troubleshoot Layer 3 VPNs (L3VPNs) and virtual router redundancy for fault-tolerant services
