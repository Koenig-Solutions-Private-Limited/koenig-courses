## What You Will Learn
- 5G New Radio (NR) physical layer fundamentals, including waveform design and beamforming techniques
- End-to-end 5G system architecture, from NG-RAN to 5GC with service-based interfaces
- Network slicing mechanisms for eMBB, URLLC, and mMTC service types
- 5G Standalone (SA) and Non-Standalone (NSA) deployment models and migration paths from 4G
- Security enhancements in 5G, such as SUCI encryption and authentication frameworks

## Who Should Attend
Network engineers, telecom analysts, RF technicians, and IT managers with basic LTE knowledge. Suited for professionals seeking entry into 5G operations or those upgrading skills for vendor-agnostic deployments; prior wireless experience helpful but not required.
