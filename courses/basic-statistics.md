## What You Will Learn
- Compute and interpret descriptive statistics including mean, median, standard deviation, and data visualization techniques like histograms.
- Grasp probability fundamentals, conditional probability, and Bayes' theorem for risk assessment in IT systems.
- Explore common probability distributions such as binomial, Poisson, and normal for modeling data events.
- Apply hypothesis testing methods like t-tests and chi-square to validate IT experiment results.
- Introduce correlation, simple linear regression, and residual analysis for predictive data trends.
