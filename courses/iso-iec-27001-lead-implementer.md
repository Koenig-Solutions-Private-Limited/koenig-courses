## What You Will Learn

- Develop and populate the Statement of Applicability, selecting Annex A controls tailored to organizational context.
- Implement operational controls, including access management, incident response, and supplier relationships.
- Prepare for certification through internal audits, management reviews, and continual improvement processes.
