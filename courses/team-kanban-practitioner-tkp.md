## What You Will Learn
- Design team workflows using the Systems Thinking Approach to Introducing Kanban (STATIK) to map value streams accurately.
- Build and maintain visual Kanban boards to represent work stages, explicit policies, and team agreements.
- Set and manage Work In Progress (WIP) limits to prevent overload and promote flow efficiency.
- Analyze flow metrics like cycle time, lead time, and throughput for data-driven process refinements.
- Handle service delivery cadence, replenishment meetings, and risk classes to ensure reliable team commitments.

## Who Should Attend
