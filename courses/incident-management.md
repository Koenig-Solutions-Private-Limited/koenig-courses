## What You Will Learn
- Implement incident logging protocols to capture detailed event data for traceability and analysis
- Apply prioritization matrices based on impact and urgency to allocate resources effectively
- Conduct initial diagnosis using diagnostic tools and escalation paths for swift containment
- Execute resolution workflows, including functional and temporary fixes with stakeholder communication
- Perform major incident reviews to identify trends, update knowledge bases, and prevent recurrences

## Who Should Attend
