## What You Will Learn
- Interpret ISO 55001 clauses and requirements for asset management systems, including strategic alignment and leadership commitments.
- Develop comprehensive audit plans, defining scope, objectives, and criteria tailored to organizational asset portfolios.
- Apply audit techniques like interviews, document reviews, and observations to assess conformance during on-site and remote audits.
- Identify, classify, and document non-conformities, opportunities for improvement, and best practices in audit reports.
- Lead audit teams, manage conflicts, and conduct closing meetings to ensure effective follow-up and continual improvement.
