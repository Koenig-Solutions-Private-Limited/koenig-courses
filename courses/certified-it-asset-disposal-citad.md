## What You Will Learn
- Implement data destruction methods like degaussing, shredding, and cryptographic erasure per NIST 800-88 guidelines.
- Navigate international regulations including GDPR, HIPAA, and WEEE for compliant IT asset retirement.
- Conduct environmental impact assessments and select certified recyclers for hardware components.
- Establish chain-of-custody documentation and perform post-disposal audits to verify security.
- Evaluate and manage third-party disposal vendors through risk assessments and SLAs.
