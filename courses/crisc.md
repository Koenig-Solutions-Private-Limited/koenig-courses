## What You Will Learn
- Identifying IT risks through frameworks like COBIT and analyzing threat scenarios across enterprise systems
- Performing quantitative and qualitative risk assessments, including vulnerability prioritization and impact modeling
- Developing and implementing risk response strategies, such as mitigation plans and residual risk evaluation
- Designing information systems controls to address identified risks, with focus on effectiveness testing
- Monitoring key risk indicators and reporting outcomes to stakeholders for continuous improvement

## Who Should Attend
