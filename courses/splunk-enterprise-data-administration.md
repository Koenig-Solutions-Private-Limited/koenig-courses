## What You Will Learn
- Configure and manage Splunk indexers, including cluster setup and data retention policies for efficient storage.
- Implement universal and heavy forwarders to onboard data from files, networks, and cloud sources securely.
- Develop advanced data parsing rules, including props.conf and transforms.conf for field extractions and routing.
- Build and accelerate data models to enhance search performance and support complex analytics queries.
- Administer access controls, role-based permissions, and summary indexing to secure sensitive data flows.
