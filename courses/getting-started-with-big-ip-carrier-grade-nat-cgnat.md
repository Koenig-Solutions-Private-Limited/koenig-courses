## What You Will Learn
- Core CGNAT concepts including deterministic and endpoint-independent NAT mappings on BIG-IP systems
- Configuring NAT pools, translation addresses, and port block allocations for large-scale subscriber endpoints
- Implementing session logging with Application Visibility and Reporting (AVR) for compliance and troubleshooting
- Setting up high availability clusters and failover for resilient CGNAT deployments
- Integrating CGNAT with DNS64 for hybrid IPv4/IPv6 environments and traffic steering
