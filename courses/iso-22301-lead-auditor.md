## What You Will Learn
- Interpret ISO 22301:2019 clauses, including leadership commitment, planning, support, and performance evaluation for BCMS.
- Apply ISO 19011 guidelines to develop audit plans, checklists, and sampling strategies tailored to BCMS scopes.
- Conduct opening meetings, evidence collection through interviews, observations, and document reviews during on-site audits.
- Analyze nonconformities, root causes, and prepare audit reports with clear findings, recommendations, and follow-up actions.
- Lead audit teams, manage conflicts, and execute certification audit processes from Stage 1 to surveillance audits.
