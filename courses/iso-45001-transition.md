## What You Will Learn
- Identify structural changes and new clauses distinguishing ISO 45001 from OHSAS 18001.
- Apply risk-based thinking and hazard identification techniques central to the standard.
- Develop leadership and worker participation plans for effective OH&S management.
- Implement operational controls, emergency preparedness, and performance monitoring processes.
- Conduct internal audits and management reviews compliant with ISO 45001 requirements.

## Who Should Attend
