## What You Will Learn
- Interpret ISO 14001:2015 clauses and EMS performance evaluation requirements for effective audit scoping.
- Develop audit plans, including risk-based sampling, checklists, and resource allocation per ISO 19011.
- Conduct opening meetings, document reviews, interviews, and site observations during on-site audits.
- Compile nonconformity reports, evaluate root causes, and recommend corrective actions.
- Manage audit follow-up, certification decisions, and continual improvement processes as a lead auditor.
