## What You Will Learn
- Configure and manage Brocade Gen 6/7 switches, including Virtual Fabrics and Access Gateways for multi-tenant isolation
- Implement advanced zoning types like Traffic Isolation Zoning (TIZ) and LSAN zoning for inter-fabric connectivity
- Perform Fabric OS firmware upgrades, license management, and high-availability setups with HA pairs
- Utilize DCFM and Brocade Network Advisor for fabric-wide monitoring, diagnostics, and performance analytics
- Troubleshoot fabric issues using RASLog, MAPS policies, and FDMI for device discovery and error resolution
